⏱️ Rules for the 3-Person Team
As previously agreed, the finishing time for the trio teams will be calculated as follows:

Leg 1: 1st runner completes the first 5K leg.

Leg 2: The remaining 2 runners start the second leg together.

Official Time: The average of the two finishing times from Leg 2.

📊 Scoring System
Points will be awarded to the 12 small teams based on their finishing positions. The main team with the highest combined total points wins the Championship.

1st: 13 pts | 2nd: 11 pts | 3rd: 10 pts

4th: 9 pts | 5th: 8 pts | 6th: 7 pts

7th: 6 pts | 8th: 5 pts | 9th: 4 pts

10th: 3 pts | 11th: 2 pts | 12th: 0 pts

In Case of a Tie (Draw): If the total points for both main teams end up in a draw, we will calculate the combined total time of all small teams for each main team. The main team with the fastest overall total time wins.

⚠️ No-Show & Penalty Rules

1️⃣ 1 Runner No-Show: The remaining partner can still run the full 10K to finish the relay. A 5-point deduction will be applied to their end result, AND the final score for this small team will be capped at a maximum of 6 points (this is to avoid a super-runner pairing up with the slowest runner and tactically creating a no-show for the slower runner to gain an advantage).

2️⃣ 2 Runners No-Show / Did Not Finish (DNF): If two bros in one relay team no-show, OR if any small team starts but cannot finish the race (DNF), it will be considered an Unfinished result. They will be placed last (0 points) and hit with an additional 5-point deduction for the main team.
