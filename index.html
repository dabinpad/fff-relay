Scoring rules & points table
Trio = leg-1 runner (full) + average of the two leg-2 runners → t_leg1 + (a+b)/2. Fastest-on-leg-1 is set by default.
No-show (NS) — tick NS next to a runner in section 1 to mark them absent. They leave the lineup, and the team forfeits a runner: Optimise then fields one solo (a teammate runs the full 10 km = 2 × 5K) that takes −5 and is capped at 6 pts. One NS → one capped solo.
DNF (toggle on a team) is placed last for 0 pts and takes an extra −5.
10K override — set a per-runner 10 km time in the roster (blank ⇒ 10K = 2×5K, no fatigue).
Lock (toggle on a team) — the optimiser keeps that exact team fixed; e.g. put your chosen 10 km runner alone on a team and lock it, then optimise the rest.
Tie on points → the team with the faster combined time wins.
