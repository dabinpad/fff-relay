Scoring rules & points table
Trio = leg-1 runner (full) + average of the two leg-2 runners → t_leg1 + (a+b)/2. Fastest-on-leg-1 is set by default.
No-show (NS) — tick NS next to a runner in section 1 to mark them absent. They leave the lineup and the team forfeits a runner. Optimise fields one capped no-show team (−5, max 6 pts) per NS, trying both and keeping the better:
  • Solo — one teammate runs the full 10 km = 2 × 5K (or the 10K override).
  • 10K-trio — the leg-1 runner runs the 10 km; his latter 5K is averaged with a leg-2 runner's 5K: 5K + (latter-5K + partner-5K)/2.
DNF (toggle on a team) is placed last for 0 pts and takes an extra −5.
10K override — set a per-runner 10 km time in the roster (blank ⇒ 10K = 2×5K, no fatigue).
Lock (toggle on a team) — the optimiser keeps that exact team fixed; e.g. put your chosen 10 km runner alone on a team and lock it, then optimise the rest.
Win % (next to each score) — a race-day Monte-Carlo win chance vs the opponent lineup currently on the board, over many races with each leg jittered ±10 s/km.
Tie on points → the team with the faster combined time wins.
